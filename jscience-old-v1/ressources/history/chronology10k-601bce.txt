Ancient World History Chronology: 10,000-601bce
10,000: Neolithic Age: Ice Age glaciers retreat; Mediterranean climate (dry
  summer) develops; also in California, SW Australia, Chile, SW Africa; also
  deserts in subtropics; domestication of plants, animals, earth, humans;
  1st in West Asia "Fertile Crescent" (wheat,barley), China (millet,rice),
  Mexico (maize,beans); 1st pottery in Japan; world population c.3 million
8500: West Asia: wheat, barley, pea, olive, sheep, goat cultivation
8000: Copper Age: 1st in West Asia; world population c.5 million

6000: Catalhoyuk, Turkey: large neolithic village [Mellaart,1961]
5500: Persia: copper smelted from malachite

5000: Central Africa (Sahel): sorghum, millet, rice, guinea fowl cultivation
4500-3000: Banpo, China: Yangshao culture; millet, pigs, dogs, red painted
  pottery, weaving, writing; earliest known neolithic site in China [1952]
4500-3500: Uruk period of Sumer: Eridu (12H), Ur (10H), Uruk (70H); writing;
  temples: Al Ubaid & Janna @Eridu, White @Uruk; Mes-Kalam-Dug tomb near Ur

  Great Sphinx [pre-5000?,West-Schoch,1992] at Giza; Menkaure built 3rd & last
