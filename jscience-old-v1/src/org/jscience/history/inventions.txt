Timeline of invention
From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia, http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_inventions.

This is a chronological list of inventions.

Note: Dates for inventions are often controversial. Inventions are often invented by several inventors around the same time, or may be invented in an impractical form many years before another inventor improves the invention into a practical form. Where there is ambiguity, the date of the first practical, fielded version of the invention is used here.
